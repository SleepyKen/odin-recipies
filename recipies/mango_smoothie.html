Mango Smoothie

A delicious mango smoothie for a hot and dry day. The sweet taste from the chilling mango will always make you want to make this smoothie every summery day

Ingredients

  • 100g Frozen Mango
  • 2 tablespoons Greek Yogurt
  • 200ml milk
  • 1 banana

Steps

  1. Cut the banana in half and throw into blender
  2. Put in 2 tablespoons of Greek Yogurt into blender
  3. Throw frozen mango into blender
  4. Pour milk until covering all ingredients
  5. Blend it for a minute
  6. Enjoy
